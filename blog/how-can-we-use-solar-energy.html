How Can We Use Solar Energy?

By Don Barnetson · June 12, 2018 · Sustainability

In recent years, solar energy has become one of the most popular forms of sustainable energy that is used around the world. Unlike unsustainable forms of energy, solar energy can provide the energy that everyone uses in one year from just one hour of sunlight. As a result, it is not surprising that the sun continues to be one of the most significant forms of sustainable energy today. While most people understand what solar energy is, they may not know what solar energy can be used for. Below are just a couple of ways that solar energy is being utilized around the world.

Solar-Powered Transportation
Because of the significant power that comes from solar energy, it is not surprising to learn that solar energy is now being used to power transportation using photovoltaic (PV) energy. Photovoltaic energy is a powerful form of energy that is created using solar energy and has enough energy to provide power for large necessities, like transportation. In 2016, the first ever solar-powered airplane successfully flew across the Pacific ocean, indicating the progress that is being made in the field of sustainable energy.

Wearable Solar Technology
In today's technology-powered society, it is becoming more common for tech companies to find new, inventive ways to keep personal electronics charged for consumers. Most recently, companies have begun introducing solar-powered chargers for personal electronics, including for cell phones, wearable fitness trackers, or personal tablets. This not only increases the places that consumers can bring their electronics but allows them to power up their devices without needing to find a power outlet.

Solar Neighborhood Lighting
For many neighborhoods across the nation, the additional costs of powering their streets can be frustrating and expensive. To help combat the issue, solar lighting is now being introduced to homeowners in neighborhoods that require lighting. In addition to a less expensive installation, solar lights do not require a power source, reducing the possibility of electric complications. Each day, the solar lights absorb energy from the sun, and use that energy to power the lights following the sunset. Not only can this reduce the cost of lighting a neighborhood, but they are easier to install and maintain.

While solar energy is not being used everywhere, the incredible progress that has been made to utilize sustainable energy in recent years indicates that it will only become more common. Using sustainable energy, like solar energy, can not only help power in a more efficient way, but it can help to reduce the damage that non-renewable energy does.
